Google Fonts

Google Fonts Knowledge supplements our font catalog with a rich library of peer-reviewed guides to the world of typography, created by typographic experts around the world. It covers universal principles of typography for any medium, with scope to delve into all of the artistic and technical aspects of modern typesetting and font technology.
All the fonts and icons in our catalog are open source and available to anyone, making beautiful typography and iconography accessible to anyone for any project. This means you can share favorites and collaborate easily with friends and colleagues. Google Fonts takes care of all the licensing and hosting, ensuring that the latest and greatest version of any font is available to everyone.
